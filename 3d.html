Renault AMC 35 Light Tank

This tank is a French Renault AMC 35 from WWII with some artistic liberties on my part. It took me roughly nine hours to model its 68,952 faces. I used constraints to get the wheels to rotate along with the tank. The constraints made animating the tank extremely easy and efficient.

GMC ACK-like Truck

This truck is a blend of the GMC ACK-353 and the Opel Blitz, both trucks in use during the Second World War. It took me about five hours to produce and has 77,629 quads.

Apartment with Crazy Floor

This project was another experiment with lighting and shading. This time, I utilized multiple light sources and types to create a dynamic scene of a city apartment at night. I found an image of an apartment online and I wanted to recreate it.

Modern Apartment with Fruit Bowl

The purpose of this project was to advance my understanding of lighting and various shading techniques in Blender. This was my first experience making a full scene in 3D with all of my own models. Most of the texturing was also done by me, with the exception of the wood used around the scene and the flooring.
