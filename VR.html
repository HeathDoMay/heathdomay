The Battle of Leipzig - VR

This VR project is a simulation of the Battle of Leipzig during the Napoleonic Wars. It features fully custom 3D models, sound effects, and programming. The user assumes control of a Prussian light infantryman fighting just north of the city of Leipzig, where Napoleon's forces are attempting to maintain a hold on the city. The simulation utilizes procedural generation of environments and feature AI-driven opponents.
